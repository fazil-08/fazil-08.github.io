About Me

I am 17 years old and was born and raised in Masalli, Azerbaijan. I completed my secondary education at Masalli District School #3, where I developed a strong foundation in mathematics and science.

I'm always eager to learn new skills and take on challenging projects that push my boundaries.

When I'm not immersed in my studies or working on projects, you can find me:
